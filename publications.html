Dr. Dechao Fu's Talk on Crop Farm Industry in Canada (in Mandarin)

Dr. Dechao (David) Fu obtained his PhD in Nankai University, Tianjin, China. He majored in chemistry. After he worked as a scientist in the Federal Government, he became an owner of a crop farm in Regina, Saskatchewan. He has been successfully farming in Canada for years. The main crops he grows are wheat, canola, peas, and barley. His audio talk on Canadian agricultural industry helps us better understand farming operation, crop inputs, such as pesticides, fertilizer, biostimulant and other aspects. He was interviewed by CBC in 2019 (Chinese farmers are shaking up Canada’s agricultural communities https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=98UzVWDoDCE&ab_channel=CBCDocs ).

Overview and prospect of agrochemical discovery in China

Abstract: China is a big agricultural country, and modern agricultural production can not do well without agricultural chemicals. After nearly 70 years of development, China's pesticide industry has gradually developed from imitation of foreign varieties to combination of imitation and independent innovation. On the occasion of the 70th anniversary of the founding of China, this paper briefly summarizes the development history of agrochemicals discovery in China, and the structure, performance, discovery process, mode of action, patent and registration, and further prospect of most pesticide products discovered and developed in China are also introduced.

Hanson Helps Chinese Pesticide Manufacturers Register Four AIs in PMRA

Since 2015, Hanson Industry Company has been a leader in bridging the Canadian crop protection market needs with Chinese Pesticide suppliers. We have successfully aided multiple Chinese pesticide manufacturers to register 4 technical products, which include glufosinate, florasulam, boscalid and s-metolachlor at PMRA, Health Canada. The registration certificate of glufosinate 200 g/L has been issued at PMRA. More generic active ingredients have been, or will be submitted to PMRA soon, which include formulations of glyphosate, glufosinate, clodinafop-propargyl 2,4-D, clethodim, pyraclostrobin and others. We also aid in the registration and commercialization of other products, such as plant growth regulators (PGRs), biostimulants, bio-pesticides, and beneficial insects.

Agrow China Top 30 Companies 2016

This is the first comprehensive China Top 30 Crop Protection Companies report, derived from primary market research throughout the regions and provinces. It is copiously illustrated throughout with totally original data. Given the current interest in China’s growing clout in the global crop protection sector, the authors believe there is further scope for both consolidation and diversification, both in the domestic market and beyond. The Chinese crop protection market is valued at US$ 5.2 billion at provincial distributor level, making it the third largest in the world. Around two-thirds of the market is generic, and domestic products account for 65%, while imported products account for 35%. The authors examine each major Chinese CP company in detail - their history and evolution, products, sales, manufacturing capabilities, market positioning and key drivers, and R&D pipeline strategies.
